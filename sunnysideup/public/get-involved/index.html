How Can You Help Us Build Happiness?

Being part of SunnySideUp means being a hands-on contributor. Whether you have time, resources, or just a willingness to show up, there is a way for you to make a direct impact.

🧤 Volunteer Your Time

Your time is perhaps our most valuable resource. We need hands for every effort—from sorting donations to directing cleanups.

How to Sign Up:

  1. Online Form: Fill out our dedicated volunteer interest form (Coming Soon) to let us know your availability and skills.
  2. Direct Contact: Call us at (Coming Soon)and ask to speak to the Volunteer Coordinator.
  3. Show Up: Keep an eye on our Instagram page (https://www.instagram.com/sunnysideupkcmo/) for scheduled cleanup days and donation sorting events!

♻️ Donate Items (Material Support)

Donations are welcome any time! Please ensure all donated items are clean, in good working condition, and usable.

  • What we need most: TBD
  • 💡 Donation Tip: Please check the expiration dates on food items before donating to ensure maximum impact!

💼 Corporate Partnerships

Do you run a local business, school, or organization? You can amplify our impact by:

  • Hosting a dedicated collection drive on your campus/premises.
  • Sponsoring a cleanup day or a large donation collection.
  • Connecting us with a group that has skills in marketing, accounting, or logistics.
