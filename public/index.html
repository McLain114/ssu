☀️ SunnySideUp KCMO (SSU)

Our Mission: Spreading Happiness, Building Community

SunnySideUp KCMO is a community-focused organization dedicated to spreading happiness and making a tangible difference in the lives of those around us.

We believe that community strength comes from supporting one another. Our efforts are focused on three core pillars:

  • 🧺 Food Donations: Ensuring that no neighbor goes hungry by collecting and distributing essential food supplies.
  • 👚 Clothing Drives: Providing dignity and warmth by collecting and distributing gently used clothing items.
  • 🌳 Community Cleanup: Working together to revitalize neighborhoods, making our shared spaces beautiful and safe for everyone.

(This bold section is inspired by our Instagram!) Our motto? We clean things. We feed people. We show up. We are NOT a cult. 👀

How Can You Help Us Build Happiness?

Being part of SunnySideUp means being a hands-on contributor. Whether you have time, resources, or just a willingness to show up, there is a way for you to make a direct impact.

🧤 Volunteer Your Time

Your time is perhaps our most valuable resource. We need hands for every effort—from sorting donations to directing cleanups.

How to Sign Up:

  1. Online Form: Fill out our dedicated volunteer interest form (Coming Soon) to let us know your availability and skills.
