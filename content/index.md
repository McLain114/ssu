# ☀️ SunnySideUp (SSU)

## Our Mission: Spreading Happiness, Building Community

SunnySideUp is a community-focused organization dedicated to **spreading happiness and making a tangible difference** in the lives of those around us.

We believe that community strength comes from supporting one another. Our efforts are focused on three core pillars:

*   🧺 **Food Donations:** Ensuring that no neighbor goes hungry by collecting and distributing essential food supplies.
*   👚 **Clothing Drives:** Providing dignity and warmth by collecting and distributing gently used clothing items.
*   🌳 **Community Cleanup:** Working together to revitalize neighborhoods, making our shared spaces beautiful and safe for everyone.
